AFFIDAVIT OF HEIRSHIP WITH WILL ATTACHED

BEFORE ME, the undersigned Notary Public, on this day personally appeared Affiants
who, after being duly sworn, according to the law, upon their oath say as follows:

1.      That we, Lawrence L. Dorsett, Donald J. Dorsett, and Diane D. Scripps are the
        sole surviving children of Genevieve B. Dorsett,Deceased, and that as such, we
        are personally acquainted with the family history and facts of heirship under the
        said Genevieve B. Dorsett, Deceased.

2.      That the said Genevieve B. Dorsett was born on April 4, 1913, in Delta County,
        State of Texas, and died on April 5, 1999, in Limestone County, State of Texas.
        The said Genevieve B. Dorsett was married one time, that being on January 13,
        1933 to Laroy Lee Dorsett, who was born on January 22, 1911, and who died on
        December 9, 1980.  To that marriage there were three children born: Lawrence L.
        Dorsett, Donald J. Dorsett, and Diane D. Scripps.  Neither Genevieve B. Dorsett
        nor Laroy Lee Dorsett ever adopted any child or children during their lifetime.

3.      That the said Genevieve B. Dorsett, Deceased, died leaving an executed last will
        and testament dated March 5, 1981, but there is no administration necessary for
        her estate since all of the debts of the said decedent, including funeral expenses
        have been paid or there are sufficient assets in her estate to pay all said debts and
        there are no federal or state inheritance taxes due against the Estate of Genevieve
        B. Dorsett for the reason that her estate consists entirely of personal property and
        the value of her estate is far less than the exemptions for federal estate tax and
        state inheritance taxes in effect as of the date of her death.  At the time of her
        death, her estate consisted of the following property:

        Checking account # 12050838 at First Mexia Bank containing
        approximately $6,000.00 after funeral expenses;

        Certificate of deposit #0452 in the name of Genevieve
        B. Dorsett, account # 9104431 at First State Bank, Coolidge
        valued at approximately $16,000.00;

        Twelve (12) burial plots in the Tehuacana Cemetary
        valued at approximately $1,200.00.

4.      Affiants, herein, are the sole and only heirs at law of the said Genevieve B.
        Dorsett, Deceased, are the sole and only persons entitled to her estate as set forth
        in the applicable provisions of the Texas Probate Code, and pursuant to the Last
        Will and Testament of said Decedent, dated March 5, 1981, a copy of which is

Last Will and Testament

THE STATE OF TEXAS, )
COUNTY OF LIMESTONE )      KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

    That I, GENEVIEVE B. DORSETT, of the County of Limestone, State
of Texas, being in good health, of sound and disposing mind and
memory and above the age of nineteen years, do make and publish this
my last will and testament hereby revoking all wills by me at anytime
heretofore made.

    FIRST:  I direct that all my just debts be paid by my Executor
hereinafter named as soon after my death as is practical, and out of
the most convenient assets of my estate and I give and grant unto my
Executor the full power and right of sale of the assets of my estate
for such purposes.

    SECOND:  I give to my three children, Donald J. Dorsett,
Lawrence L. Dorsett, and Diane D. Schripps, all the property, real,
personal and mixed of every kind and character which I own at my
death, in equal shares, in fee simple, to dispose of as they may
desire.

    THIRD:  I hereby appoint my son, Donald J. Dorsett, as
Independent Executor of this my last will and direct that no bond be
required of him and that no other action shall be had in the County
Court in relation to the settlement of my estate than the probating
and recording of this my will and the return of statutory inventory,
appraisement and list of claims of my estate and all claims due or
owing by me at the time of my death.

    This I make and publish as my last will hereunto signing and
subscribing my name this the 5th day of March, 1981.

[handwritten signature]
Testatrix

The above instrument was now here published as her last will
and signed and subscribed by Genevieve B. Dorsett, the Testatrix,
in our presence, and we, at her request, in her presence and in the
presence of each other, sign and subscribe our names hereto as at-
testing witnesses.
