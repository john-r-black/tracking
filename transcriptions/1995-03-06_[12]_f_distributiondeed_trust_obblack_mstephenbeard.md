DISTRIBUTION DEED

(John Pierce Black Family Trust)

THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF FREESTONE

NATIONSBANK OF TEXAS, N.A. (successor in interest to Citizens National Bank of Waco), as co-Trustee of the JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977, created by Trust Agreement dated July 19, 1977, signed by John Pierce Black, herein "Grantor", for a sufficient consideration received, does hereby GRANT, TRANSFER and CONVEY, unto M. STEPHEN BEARD, as successor co-Trustee of the JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977, herein "Grantee", the following described property in Freestone County, Texas (the "Property"):

Tract #1:

All that certain lot, tract or parcel of land, a part of the Sarah McAnulty Survey, in Freestone County, Texas, on the North Side of Tehuacana Creek, consisting of approximately 85.30 acres of land, more or less, and being the same tract described as Tract #1 in a deed from Olena Bounds Black, Individually and as Independent Executrix for the Estate of J. P. Black, Deceased to the "John Pierce Black Family Trust of 1977" dated May 23, 1979 and recorded in Volume 531, Page 500 of the Deed Records of Freestone County, Texas.

Tract #2:

All that certain tract or parcel of land lying and being situated in Freestone County, Texas, about one and one-half miles East from the town of Wortham, consisting of approximately 33.61 acres of land, more or less, out of the Wm. Richie Survey and being the same tract described as Tract #2 in a deed from Olena Bounds Black, Individually and as Independent Executrix for the Estate of J. P. Black, Deceased to the "John Pierce Black Family Trust of 1977" dated May 23, 1979 and recorded in Volume 531, Page 500 of the Deed Records of Freestone County, Texas.

This conveyance is made subject to all restrictions, encumbrances, liens, rights-of-way, easements, outstanding mineral and royalty interests, and other matters which may affect title to the Property, if any, which are of record in the Office of the County Clerk of said county, and to tenants in possession under unrecorded leases, whether written or oral, and to any matter which may be shown by an inspection or survey of the Property, to the extent and only to the extent that the same are valid and subsisting and affect the Property.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the Property, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto and in any wise belonging, subject as aforesaid, unto Grantee, Grantee's successors, legal representatives and assigns, forever. This conveyance is made for purposes of evidencing the change in the co-Trustee of the John Pierce Black Family Trust of 1977 and is therefore made without warranty or representation, either expressed or implied.

OLENA BOUNDS BLACK, as co-Trustee of the JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977 joins herein for purposes of acknowledging this transfer and designation of M. Stephen Beard, as Successor Co-Trustee.

EXECUTED this 6th day of March, 1995.
