[MARGIN: J. H. Bounds / & wife / to Deed / H. C. Dunbar / aug 27/06]

State of Texas }
Limestone County } Know all men by these presents that James H. Bounds and Margaret E Bound his wife for and in consideration of the sum of three hundred dollars to us in hand paid by H. Clay Dunbar of said county and State the receipt whereof is here acknowledged have this day bargained and sold and by these presents do bargain sell alien transfer and convey to the said H. Clay Dunbar all our right title interest and claims in and to the following

[--- Page Break: DR-00048-00373-02.png ---]

[STAMP: 374]

tract or parcel of land lying and being situated in said county & State and described as follows be a part of the Sarah McAnulty League conveyed by Thomas Hare and wife to the said James H. Bounds and by said Bounds and wife to the said H Clay Dunbar and more particular described as follows to wit. Beginning 26 & 1/2 vrs from the Southeast corner of said McAnulty league surveying on the south Boundry line to a stake thence North so as to include two hundred acres thence East to the line of said survey from Hare to Bound thence south to the place of beginning to as to include two hundred acres off of the South end of said survey from the said Hare to the said Bound as described in the deed from Hare to Bound and the said Dunbar their heirs and assigns is to forever hold said two hundred acres of land at his disposal warranted and defended by the said J Hill Bounds & wife as witness there hands and seal this third day of August in the year of our Lord 1869 using scroll for seal

attest                              James H Bound (seal)
B. D. Loper                        Margaret E Bound. (seal)
John L. Wolfe

Revenue Stamp to the amount of 50 cents and duly cancelled.

---

State of Texas }
Limestone Co. } Before me this day personally appeared James H. Bound to me well known and acknowledged that he signed the foregoing deed to H. Clay Dunbar for all the purposes and consideration therein expressed and also personally appeared Margaret E Bounds wife of said James H. Bounds and she being examined by me separate and apart from her said husband she acknowledged that she signed the foregoing deed for all of the purposes therein stated voluntary and with out any compulsion on the part of her

husband and wished not to retract it to all which witness my hand & official seal at Springfield this the 13th day of Nov A.D. 1869

J. B. Vallandingham
