THE STATE OF TEXAS *
COUNTY OF LIMESTONE *

WHEREAS, it is recognized that J. P. BLACK and OLENA BOUNDS BLACK, as husband and wife, were the owners of a residence located at Route 1, Box 166, Wortham, Texas, 76693, which residence represented a community property asset of the marriage of Mr. and Mrs. Black; and
 
WHEREAS, it is recognized that said community property residence was physically moved upon separate property previously inherited by Mrs. Black being described in the attached Exhibit "A", hereto, which Exhibit "A" is incorporated herein by this reference for all purposes as though the same were fully set forth at length herein;

NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the sum of TEN AND NO/100 ($10.00) DOLLARS cash, and other good and valuable consideration, the JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977, dated July 19, 1977, acting and represented herein by and through its duly authorized Trustees, the recipient of those assets constituting the Estate of J. P. Black, Deceased, by reason of his death, on November 14, 1978, and to effect a partition of the Estate of J. P. Black, Deceased, pursuant to a Partition Agreement, dated May 23, 1979, does and hereby BARGAIN, SELL, ASSIGN, TRANSFER, AND CONVEY and has hereby BARGAINED, SOLD, ASSIGNED, TRANSFERRED, AND CONVEYED, any and all right, title and interest it might otherwise have in and to said residence and improvements, and further, does hereby quit-claim unto Olena Bounds Black all of its right, title and interest, if any, in and to the property described in the attached Exhibit "A" hereto.

EXECUTED this 23rd day of May, 1979.

JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977
Dated July 19, 1977 
BY: CITIZENS NATIONAL BANK OF WACO,
Waco, Texas, Co-Trustee

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared FRED WORLEY, known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that he executed the same as the act and deed of THE CITIZENS NATIONAL BANK, and as the Trust Officer thereof, for the purposes and consideration therein expressed, and in the capacity therein stated.
GIVEN UNDER MY HAND AND SEAL OF OFFICE, this 25th day of May, 1979.

Notary Public in and for McLennan County, Texas

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared OLENA BLACK, known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that she executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed.
GIVEN UNDER MY HAND AND SEAL OF OFFICE, this 23rd day of May, 1979.

Notary Public in and for McLennan County, Texas
