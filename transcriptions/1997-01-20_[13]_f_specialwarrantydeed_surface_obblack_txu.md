SPECIAL WARRANTY DEED

THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF FREESTONE

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

That OLENA BLACK, a widow, RR 1, Box 243, Wortham, TX 76693-9801 ("GRANTOR"), for and in consideration of the sum of TEN AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($10.00), and other good and valuable consideration to her in hand paid by TEXAS UTILITIES ELECTRIC COMPANY, Energy Plaza, 1601 Bryan St., Dallas, TX 75201, ("GRANTEE"), the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, has granted, sold and conveyed, and by these presents does grant, sell and convey unto the GRANTEE all that certain property lying and being situated in Freestone County, Texas, more particularly described in Exhibit "A" and depicted on Exhibit "B" attached hereto and made a part hereof.

It is understood and agreed that GRANTOR, her heirs, successors and assigns shall be responsible for the erection and maintenance of its electric fence to be built on the new surveyed boundary line of the property described in Exhibit "A". GRANTEE shall be responsible for erection and maintenance of its fence within the limits of the property described in Exhibit "A".

It is further understood and agreed to that GRANTOR reserves all oil, gas, and other minerals located in, on and under the property described in Exhibit "A" herein. GRANTOR conveys to GRANTEE all interest in coal and lignite located in, on and under the property described in Exhibit "A" herein.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above-described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said TEXAS UTILITIES ELECTRIC COMPANY so long as said premises are used for a rail line to serve GRANTEE's Big Brown Steam Electric Station and GRANTEE abandons said premises; and it is expressly understood that in the event the premises hereinabove described permanently cease to be used for rail transportation to serve Big Brown Steam Electric Station and GRANTEE abandons said premises, then in that event, this conveyance shall be null and void and the said premises, shall absolutely revert to the GRANTOR herein, her heirs, successors, or assigns, without the necessity of any other act or action on the part of the GRANTOR, provided that GRANTEE shall have the right to remove, upon its abandonment of the premises, all improvements constructed, installed, or maintained by GRANTEE on the premises, including rails, tracks, ties, fencing, gates, and cattle guards, from the premises.

GRANTOR, for the consideration and subject to the above-stated exceptions, grants, sells and conveys to GRANTEE the property, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging, to have and hold it to GRANTEE, GRANTEE's heirs, executors, administrators, successors, or assigns forever. GRANTOR binds GRANTOR and GRANTOR's successors to warrant and forever defend all and singular the property to GRANTEE and GRANTEE's heirs, executors, administrators, successors, and assigns against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof, except as to the above-stated exceptions, when the claim is by, through, or under GRANTOR but not otherwise.

EXECUTED this 20th day of January, 1997.
OLENA BLACK
