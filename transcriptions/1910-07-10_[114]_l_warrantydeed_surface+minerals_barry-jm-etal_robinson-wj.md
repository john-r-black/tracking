[JM Barry et al
to Deed
WJ Robinson]

The State of Texas County of Limestone} Know all men by these Presents:
That we, J. M. Barry and wife, A. B. Barry, Barry J. A. Barry, S. N. Barry, W. M. Barry, Lou Bounds and her husband, J. M. Bounds, Mattie Bounds and her husband M. S. Bounds, Mildred Magness and her husband, Joe Magness, of the County of Limestone in the state aforesaid, for and in consideration of the sum of five hundred and thirty five + 5/100 Dollars to us paid and secured to be paid by W. J. Robinson, as follows: One hundred and seven Dollars and 5/100 Cash in hand the receipt whereof we hereby acknowledge and his three certain Promissory notes bearing even date herewith and described as follows to wit: each for the sum of $154.80 each due and payable respectively on January 1st 1911 January 1st 1912 and January 1st 1913, and a Vendors lien is retained against hereinafter described land to secure the payment of each of said notes have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and Convey unto the said W. J. Robinson of the County of Limestone State of Texas All that certain lot, tract, or parcel of land, lying and being situated in Limestone County, Texas on the waters of Tehuacana Creek and being 4 3 acres more or less and being a part of the P. Varela Survey. Said 4 3 acres is described by Metes and bounds as follows to wit: Beginning at the most Northwesterly corner of the Original J. M. Barry tract thence S 88 648 vrs. to N. W. Corner of Wm. M. Barry tract thence east with the North line of said Wm. M. Barry tract 3 + 6 vrs to D. W. Corner of Lot No. 6 thence north with west line of said Lot no. 6 65 + vrs to N. W. Corner of same thence E 2 3 vrs to S. W. Corner of Lot No. 1 thence north with west line line of said Lot No. 1 37 vrs to an inside Ell Corner of said Original Barry tract thence N 87 W 508 vrs to the place of beginning containing 4 3 acres of land more or less To have and to hold the above described premises together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said W. J. Robinson his heirs and assigns forever and we do hereby bind ourselves our heirs executors and administrators to warrant and forever defend all and singular the said premises unto the said W. J. Robinson his heirs and assigns against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof. But it is expressly agreed and stipulated that the Vendor lien is retained against the above described property premises and improvements untill the above described notes and all interest thereon are fully paid according to their face and tenor effect and reading when this deed shall become absolute.

Witness our hands at Wortham, Texas this 10th day of July A.D. 1910
J. M. Barry  I. B. Barry  J. A. Barry  Lou Bounds  W. M. Barry  M. S. Bounds  Mattie Bounds  Mildred Magness  M. Bounds S. N. Barry  Joe Magness
