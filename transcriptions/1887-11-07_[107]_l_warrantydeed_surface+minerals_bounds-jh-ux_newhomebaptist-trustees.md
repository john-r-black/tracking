J. H. Bounds and wife to J. M. Brown et al. Trustees for Colored New Home Baptist

THE STATE OF TEXAS,
COUNTY OF Limestone Know all Men by These Presents:
THAT WE, J. H. Bounds and wife M. E. Bounds of the County of Limestone and State aforesaid, for and in consideration of the sum of Thirty Two DOLLARS, to us in hand paid by J. M. Brown, Frank Gordon, George Brown, Trustees of the Colored New Home Baptist Church Thirty Two Dollars cash in hand paid the receipt of which hereby acknowledge do. the land to be used fore Church, School and Grave yard purpose.

have GRANTED, SOLD AND CONVEYED, and by these Presents do GRANT, SELL AND CONVEY unto the said J. M. Brown, George Brown Et al, trustees and their successors and office of the County of Limestone and State of Texas all that certain lot, tract or parcel of land described as follows: Situated about five miles N. East of Mexia on the East side of the J.N.C. Rail Road in Boyd County & State afar. a part of the Sarah McAnulty Headright Survey. Beginning at the South East corner of J. H. Bounds Home stead tract and from stake in Public road at S. D. Bounds S. H. corner thence a Mesq. 10 in brs N 12 1/4 W — thence North with S. J. Bounds fence & and Home stead track 150 vrs and a from Stake thence a Mesq. Bush brs N 70 E. 1 vrs On Elm 8 brs N 88 1/2 W 25 1/4 vrs. thence West 70 vrs to an from Stake thence an Elm 3 in brs N 59 W 1 1/2 vrs do 8 in dry brs S 11 1/2 E 5 1/2 vrs. thence South 150 vrs to an from stake on the South Boundary line of said Home S. tract thence a Mesq. 10 in brs S 1 1/4 W. 14 3/4 vrs On Elm 10 in brs North 41 1/4 vrs thence East 70 vrs to the place of beginning Containing Two acres of land.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said J. M. Brown, Et al. their Successors heirs and assigns forever; and We do hereby bind our heirs, executors and administrators, to WARRANT AND FOREVER DEFEND, all and singular the said premises unto the said J. M. Brown, Et al. and their Successors heirs and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same, or any part thereof. But it is expressly agreed and stipulated that the Vendors lien is retained against the above described property, premises, and improvement, until the above described note and all interest there on are fully paid according to face therof effect when charged shall become absolute, it is dividing security for the said note & the above instrument. With the said
WITNESS Our hands at this 7 day of November A. D. 1887.

Signed and Delivered in Presence of
J. H. Bounds
M. E. Bounds
.10

THE STATE OF TEXAS,
COUNTY OF Freestone BEFORE ME, Warren Allegra a Justice of the Peace and Ex officio Notary Public in and for Freestone County, Texas on this day personally appeared J. H. Bounds and M. E. Bounds wife of J. H. Bounds both known to me, to be the persons whose names are subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that they executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed. And the said M. E. Bounds having been examined me privily and apart from her husband and having the same by me fully explained to her, she, the said M. C. Bounds acknowledged such instrument to be her act and deed, and she declared she had willingly signed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed, and that she did not wish to retract it.
GIVEN under my hand and seal of office, this 7th day of November A. D. 1887

(L.S.)
Warren Allegra
J.P. & Ex officio Notary Public

Filed for Record the 27 day of November A. D. 1887 at 7 o'clock P. M., and recorded the 28th day of November A. D. 1887 at 3 o'clock, P.M.
By A. J. S[ILLEGIBLE] Deputy.
W. F. Brown
Clerk County Court, Limestone County, Texas.
