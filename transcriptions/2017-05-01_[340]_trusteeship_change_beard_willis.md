**TRUSTEE RESIGNATION AND APPOINTMENT**
**OF SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE**

</center>

M. Stephen Beard hereby resigns as Trustee of the John Pierce Black and Olena Bounds
Black Estate Trust No. 1 created under that certain trust instrument dated July 18, 1977, and
executed by John Pierce Black and Olena Bounds Black, as Settlors, and John Marion Black and
The Citizens National Bank of Waco, Waco, Texas, as Co-Trustees, and provides notice of such
resignation to John Marion Black, Kay Lynn Ramsey Black, John Ramsey Black (individually
and on behalf of Matthew Holland Black, his minor child), John Paul Black, Brittany Lachelle
Black, Von Ramsey Black, Margaret Kate Black and Joel Matthew Black (collectively, the
"Beneficiaries"). Article X of said trust provides that the Beneficiaries may appoint a Trustee if
there is a vacancy in the trusteeship. M. Stephen Beard's resignation creates a vacancy in the
trusteeship. In light of said vacancy and pursuant to said power, the Beneficiaries hereby appoint
D. Fred Willis as Trustee of the John Pierce Black and Olena Bounds Black Estate Trust No. 1.

This Trustee Resignation and Appointment of Successor Trustee may be executed and
exchanged by e-mail or facsimile document transmission and an e-mailed copy of facsimile
document signature shall be the equivalent of an original.

Executed effective May 1, 2017.

[Signature: *M. Stephen Beard*]
M. Stephen Beard

[Signature: *John Marion Black*]
John Marion Black

[Signature: *Kay Lynn Ramsey Black*]
Kay Lynn Ramsey Black
