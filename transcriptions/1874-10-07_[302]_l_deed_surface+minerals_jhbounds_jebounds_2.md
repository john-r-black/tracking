[MARGIN: J. H. Bounds / To Deed / J. E. Bounds]

The State of Texas  }  Know all men by these presents that
Limestone County    }

I J.H. Bounds of the above County and State have this day for and in consideration of the Sum of one Dollar to me in hand paid by J. E. Bounds of the Same County and State aforesaid granted bargained & sold and do by these presents give grant bargain sell and convey to the said J. E. Bounds and his heirs and assigns forever the following described tract or parcel of Land Situated in the above named County and State and being

[--- PAGE BREAK: DR-0000F-00584-02.png ---]

585

√ a part of the John Boyd League Beginning at four hundred varas west of the South East corner of said Jno Boyd League a stake in the south line of said League Thence North with J. V. Bounds west line to Tehuacana Creek about 1900 varas Thence up said creek with its meanderings to the North east corner of a 150 acre survey sold by Faster to Shown and thee by Shown to Alkisson & Faster Thence South 1664 varas to a stake in the south line of said Jno Boyd League and the south east corner of the above refered to 150 acre survey Thence East with Jno Boyd's South League line 500 varas to the place of beginning containing one hundred and fifty eight acres (158) more or less. To have and to hold to them the said J. E. Bounds and his heirs and assigns forever and I the said J.H. Bounds do for myself my heirs and assigns agree and hereby bind myself my heirs and assigns to warrant and forever defend the title to said described tract or parcel of land to him the said J. E. Bounds his heirs and assigns against the claim or claims of all persons claiming by or through me or my heirs and against the claim or claims of all persons legally claiming the same In Testimony whereof I have hereunto Set my name and affixed my seal this the 7th day of October A.D. 1874.

State of Texas  }
Limestone County}                    J.H. Bounds    Seal

Before me S. D. Walker clerk of the County Court in and for said County personally appear'd J.H. Bounds who is to me personally known and acknowledged that he signed executed and delivered the foregoing instrument of writing for the purposes and consideration therein specified Witness my official seal and signature at my office in the town of Groesbeck this the 2nd day of Octy A.D. 1877.

S. D. Walker
Clerk C. C. L. C.

[SEAL]

I hereby certify that the foregoing is a true copy of the original Deed and certificate of acknowledgement filed in my office for record Febuary 26th 1877 at 9 1/2 oclock A.M. and duly recorded Mch. 1st 1877 at 3 oclock P.M.

S. D. Walker
C. C. L. C. Texas
