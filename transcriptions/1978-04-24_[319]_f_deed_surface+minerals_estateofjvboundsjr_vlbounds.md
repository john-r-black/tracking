**DEED**

THE STATE OF TEXAS )
                   )  KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS THAT:
COUNTY OF FREESTONE.)

WHEREAS, The Estate of J. V. Bounds, Jr., Deceased, now pending in the County Court of Freestone County, Texas, No. 3217, owns an interest in the real property described on the page attached and designated as Exhibit A; and

WHEREAS, the undersigned, Oliver A. Bounds, the duly appointed, qualified and acting Administrator with Will Annexed of the Estate, made Application to the Court for an order to sell the property; and

WHEREAS, the Court made and entered an Order, dated April 5, 1978, directing the sale of the property, after which and on April 10, 1978, the undersigned, pursuant to such Order, sold the property at private sale to Virgil L. Bounds, the Grantee herein; and

WHEREAS, the undersigned filed the Report of Sale in this proceeding on April 10, 1978 after which date such sale was in all respects confirmed by a Decree of the Court entered on April 20, 1978; and

WHEREAS, the Grantee has complied with the terms of such sale.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Oliver A. Bounds, for and in consideration of the sum of $20,000.00, paid to me by the Grantee, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, have GRANTED, SOLD AND CONVEYED and by these presents do GRANT, SELL AND CONVEY unto the Grantee, all of the real property in Freestone and Navarro Counties, Texas, described in the attached Exhibit A.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging, unto the Grantee, his heirs and assigns, forever.

EXECUTED this [handwritten:] 24th day of April, 1978.
