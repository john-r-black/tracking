THE STATE OF TEXAS, COUNTY OF LIMESTONE.} KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:
That I, M.S.Bounds, of the County of Limestone, State of Texas, for and in consideration of the sum of TEN ($10.00) DOLLARS, and other good and valuable consideration, to me in hand paid by Mrs.Mattie Bounds, wife of M.S.Bounds, as follows: $10.00, cash in hand paid, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged and confessed, and the further consideration in payment of money loaned and advanced to me by the saidMrs.MattieBounds out of her separate estate and property; and the further consideration the love and affection I have for my wife, the said Mrs.Mattie Bounds,have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and Convey, unto the said Mrs.MattieBounds, in her own separate right and as her own separate property, of the County of Limestone, State of Texas, all that certain lot, tract or parcel of land located in the counties as hereinafter stated;

FIRST TRACT: Consisting of 94 acres of land, out of the Sarah McAnulty Survey in Limestone County, Texas, and described by metes and bounds as follows: BEGINNING at the N.E.corner of the J.H.Bounds homestead tract, rock for corner from which a P.O. 9 in. brs.
