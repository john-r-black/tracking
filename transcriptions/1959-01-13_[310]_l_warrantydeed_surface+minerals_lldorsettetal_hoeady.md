The State of Texas,            Know All Men by These Presents:
County of LIMESTONE

That we, L.L.Dorsett and wife, Mrs.Genevieve B Dorsett

of the County of Limestone        State of Texas        for and in consideration
of the sum of    Fifty Five Hundred & No/100
                 ( $5500.00 )                                        DOLLARS

to us in hand paid by cash, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged,

as follows:

$5500.00 cash in hand in full paid by Mrs.H.O.Eady

have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and Convey, unto the said

Mrs.H.O.Eady

of the County of Limestone        State of Texas        all that certain
tract or parcel of land situated in the City of Tehuacana,Limestone,
Texas,and more particularly described as follows:

Being a Lot on the S.E.corner of the South half of Block 23 in
the town of Tehuacana, and described thus:
BEGINNING at the S.E. corner of Block 23,and continuing
West 50 feet, Then continuing North the full length of the lot
until intercepted by alley,Thence East to the east line of
said Block 23, Thence South to place of beginning.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the
rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said

Mrs.H.O.Eady, her
heirs and assigns forever and we do hereby bind our selves, our
heirs, executors and administrators, to Warrant and Forever Defend, all and singular the said premises
unto the said

Mrs.H.O.Eady, her

heirs and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming, or to claim the same, or any
part thereof.

WITNESS our hands at Tyler, Texas
this 13th day of January 19 59
Witnesses at Request of Grantor:

L.L. Dorsett
Mrs Genevieve B. Dorsett
