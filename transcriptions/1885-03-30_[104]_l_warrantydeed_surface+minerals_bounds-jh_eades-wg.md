The State of Texas County of Limestone } Know all men by these presents - That I J. H. G. Eades promissory notes of even date with this deed payable to J. H. Bounds in the sum of thirty dollars - said note made due and payable on the 30th day of May 1885, also one promissory note by said Eades of even date with this deed payable to said Bounds in the sum of one hundred and thirty five dollars with ten per cent interest from date said note due and payable March 30th 1886, also in the further consideration of of the said W. G. Eades paying or causing to be paid a certain promissory note given by [BLANK] for the sum of one hundred and fifty dollars with interest - at [BLANK] per cent from the [BLANK] th day of [BLANK] 188 which note bears date the [BLANK] th day of [BLANK] A.D. 188 and is made due and payable on the [BLANK] day of [BLANK] A.D. 188 to [BLANK] and is [BLANK] now owned and held by [BLANK] and carries with it - the vendors lien on the premises hereby conveyed. Now in consideration of the said W. G. Eades paying off and fully satisfying said three notes above described I do by these presents sell and convey unto him the said W. G. Eades all the right title and interest in and to the the following tract or lot of land situated in the town of Tehuacana Limestone County Texas and Known as part of the John Boyd League and is that part of said League as is shown as Block No 19 by the map or diagram of the Town of Tehuacana and contains Two acres of land being the same Block of Land on which P.
