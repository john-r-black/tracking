[J. M. Barry et as
Deed
W.L. & W.S. Adams
May 11th 1910]

The State of Texas County of Limestone} Know all men by these Presents: That we, J. M. Barry and wife, A. B. Barry, and J. A. Barry and wife, Ora Barry, Lou Bounds, joined by her husband, J. M. Bounds, Mattie Bounds, joined by her husband, M. S. Bounds, W. M. Barry and wife, Bobbie Barry, Walter Barry and wife, Lockie Barry, Mildred Magness, joined by her husband, Joe Magness, of the County of Limestone State of Texas for and in consideration of the sum of Fifty two hundred and fifty ($5250.00) Dollars; to us in hand paid by W.L. Adams and W.S. Adams as follows: Cash in hand paid, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, (the above named J. A. Barry, Lou Bounds (nee Barry) Mattie Bounds (nee Barry) W. M. Barry, Walter Barry and Mildred Magness (nee Barry) are the only Children and heirs at law of Joanna Barry a former and deceased wife of J. M. Barry,) have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and convey unto the said W.L. Adams and W.S. Adams of the County of Limestone State of Texas all that certain Lot, Tracts or Parcel of land lying and being situate in Limestone County, Texas, out of three different surveys but in one body, being 162 acres of the Pedro Varela Eleven Leagues Grant, 96 acres out of the Lipscomb Norvelle survey, and eighty acres out of the John Boyd League aggregating three hundred and thirty eight acres (338) acres of land and described in one body as follows: Beginning at a rock at the point of intersection of the Mexia and Tehuacana road, which said point is 1025 varas west and 10 varas south from the southeast corner of the John Boyd league, which is also the northeast corner of the Lipscomb Norvelle survey; thence south 11° east, with the center of the Mexia public road 394 varas: thence Continuing with the center of said road south 45° east 227 varas intersect the east line of said P. Varela 11 Leagues Grant: thence Continuing with center of said road and Varela line south 35° east 583 varas to the southeast corner of the Darby King tract: thence south 55° west 1484 varas thence north 25° west 64 1/2 varas to the northwest corner of Holts tract. thence south 55 west 15 varas to Milanias southeast corner: thence north 4 1/2 west with Milanias east line 62 varas to the southeast corner of a 40 acre tract theretofore sold to Robinson out of said Barry lands. thence west with the south line of said Robinson tract 292 1/2 varas to his southeast corner. thence north with said Robinsons east line 694 varas to the south line of the Buttle tract. thence east with said south line 42 1/2 varas to the southeast corner of said Buttle tract: thence north with the east line of same 654 varas to the center of Tehuacana road: thence west with the center of said road 56 varas to the southeast corner of Bounds 72 acre tract which is included in the conveyance: thence north with the west line of said 72 acre tract 565 varas to the center of the channel of Little Tehuacana Creek: thence down said Creek with its meanders to the point where the public road intersects same. (Said point is the quick corner of a 1,960 acre tract conveyed to J. M. Barry by J. D. Bounds.) thence south 51° east with the center of Public road 410 varas to the northeast corner of said 72 acre tract, which is also the east corner of said 1,960 acre tract. thence south with the center of road 626 varas to the place of beginning. To Have and to Hold the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said W.L. Adams and W.S. Adams their heirs and assigns forever, and we do hereby bind ourselves, our heirs executors and administrators, to Warrant and forever Defend all and singular the said premises unto the said W.L. Adams and W. S. Adams, their heirs and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof. Witness our hands at Mexia Texas, this 22d day of March, 1910.

J. M. Bounds
Lou Bounds
J. M. Barry.
Mrs. A. B. Barry.
J. A. Barry
M. S. Bounds
William M. Barry
Mrs. Ora Barry.
Walter B. Barry.
Mrs. Lockie Barry.
Mattie Bounds.
Mrs. Bobbie Barry.
Joe Madness
Mildred Magness
