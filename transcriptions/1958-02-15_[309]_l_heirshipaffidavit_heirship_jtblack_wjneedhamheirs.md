**AFFIDAVIT OF HEIRSHIP AND MARITAL HISTORY**

STATE OF TEXAS

COUNTY OF LIMESTONE

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority on this day personally appeared JOHN T. BLACK, affiant, and on his oath says that he is over 21 years of age and is not incapacitated in anyway. That he is personally acquainted with Mrs. W. J. Needham, whose first name is, MARY ELLEN and knows of his own knowledge that she has owned and lived on a lot or tract of land in the town of Tehuacana, Limestone County, Texas, that is located on the east end of the south half of Block 23 and said lot being bound on the east by South First Street and on the south by Wade Street in the City of Tehuacana. I know of my own personal knowledge that the Needhams or their successors and grantees have owned such property since 1921 and that during this time,they have used and occupied said land exercising all the rights of ownership such as keeping it fenced, using it for pasture and garden purposes and paying the taxes thereon and that during all this time, there have never been any claims or acts of adverse ownership by anyone in regard to this property. During all such period, the possession by the Needhams and their grantees of such land has been peaceable, not being interrupted by adverse suit to recover the estate or otherwise interrupt the ~~adverse~~ claim of user.

Mr. W. J. Needham was married once and only once and that time to Mrs. W. J. (MARY ELLEN) Needham, and Mrs. W.J. (MARY ELLEN) Needham was married once and only once and that time to W. J. Needham.

W. J. Needham died in the year 1952 and that at the time of his death, he and Mrs. W.J. (MARY ELLEN) Needham were husband and wife. During the marriage, two children were born to them, one boy and one girl. The girl died in childhood but the boy is alive and his name is Virgil Needham, who is a resident of 2631 E. 35TH STREET, TULSA, OKLAHOMA.

No children were adopted by the Needhams during their marriage. W. J. Needham died without a will and there has been no administration on his estate and no need therefor.

W. J. Needham and Will J. Needham are one and the same person.

EXECUTED this 15 day of February, 1958.

John R. Black
