WARRANTY DEED

Date:    February 26, 1993

Grantor:  Genevieve Black Dorsett, a single person

Grantor's Mailing Address:  c/o Donald J. Dorsett, 2202 Barak Lane,
Bryan, Tx. 77802

Grantee:  Donald J. Dorsett, as Trustee of the Genevieve B. Dorsett
Family Trust of 1992, created under instrument dated December 24, 1992

Grantee's Mailing Address:  2202 Barak Lane, Bryan, Tx. 77802

Consideration:  Love and affection which Grantor has and bears unto
and towards her children, Diane D. Scripps, Lawrence Lee Dorsett and
Donald John Dorsett, being the beneficiaries of the Genevieve Black
Dorsett Family Trust of 1992

Property:  Property, including any improvements:

Being a part of the land described in a deed dated May 17, 1976 from
J.W. Swint and Bertha Swint to Alden Garber and Sharon Garber, said
deed being of record in Volume 623, Page 735, Deed Records of Limestone
County, Texas and said tract of land being more specifically described
as follows:

    BEGINNING at the Southeast corner of the tract of land described
    in a deed from J.W. Swint, et ux to Alden Garber, et ux, said deed
    being of record in Volume 623, Page 735 of the Deed Records of
    Limestone County, Texas;

    THENCE West 100 feet to a point;

    THENCE North 137 feet to a point;

    THENCE East 100 feet to a point;

    THENCE South 137 feet to a point; said point being the place of
    beginning.

Reservations from and Exceptions to Conveyance and Warranty:

    SUBJECT to any and all restrictions, covenants, condi-
    tions and easements, if any, relating to the hereinabove
    described Property, but only to the extent that they are
    still in force and effect, visible, or shown of record in
    the hereinabove mentioned County and State and all zoning
    laws, regulations, and ordinances of municipal or other
    governmental authorities, if any, relating to the herein-
    above described Property.

Grantor, for the consideration and subject to the reservations
from and exceptions to conveyance and warranty, grants, gives, and
conveys to Grantee, the above described interest in the Property,
together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in
any wise belonging, to have and hold it to Grantee, Grantee's succes-
sors or assigns forever. Grantor binds Grantor and Grantor's heirs,
executors, administrators, and successors to warrant and forever defend
all and singular the above described interest in the Property to
Grantee and Grantee's successors and assigns against every person
whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof,
except as to the reservations from and exceptions to conveyance and
warranty.

[signature]
Genevieve Black Dorsett
