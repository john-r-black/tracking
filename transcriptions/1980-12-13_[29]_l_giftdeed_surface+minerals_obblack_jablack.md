STATE OF TEXAS *
* DEED OF GIFT
COUNTY OF LIMESTONE *

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

That I, OLENA BLACK, of the County of Limestone, State of Texas, hereinafter referred to as "Grantor", for and in consideration of the love and affection I have and bear towards my son, James A. Black, have granted, bargained and conveyed and by these presents do grant, bargain and convey unto James A. Black of the County of Limestone, State of Texas, hereinafter referred to as "Grantee", the remaining undivided 1/2 interest in the hereinafter described property situated in Limestone County, Texas, said property being more particularly described as follows:

BEING 18.00 acres out of the Sarah McAnulty Survey, A-19, and lying on the west side of the H & TC Railroad right-of-way, and being all of the land once owned by the Estate of T. A. Bounds on said West side of railroad in said survey, said land described as the FIFTH TRACT out of the 224.00 acres conveyed by one certain Warranty Deed from M. S. Bounds to Olena Black, dated October 2, 1972, recorded in the Limestone County Deed Records.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto and anywise belonging unto said Grantee, Grantee's heirs, legal representatives, successors and assigns forever, and Grantor does hereby bind herself, her heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns to WARRANT AND FOREVER DEFEND all and singular the said premises unto the said Grantee, his heirs, successors, legal representatives and assigns against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same of any part thereof.

EXECUTED this 13th day of December, 1980 
OLENA BLACK
 

THE STATE OF TEXAS * 
* 
COUNTY OF FREESTONE *
 
BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared OLENA BLACK, known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that she executed the same for the purposes and considerations therein expressed.
GIVEN UNDER MY HAND AND SEAL OF OFFICE this 13th day of December, 1980.

Rebecca Strange 
Notary Public in and for Freestone County, Texas
My commission expires: 2-5-81
