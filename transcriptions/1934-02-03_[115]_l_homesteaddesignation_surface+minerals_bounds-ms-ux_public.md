***975***

                        M. S. Bounds et ux
                    2-8-34 to Homestead Designation
                        (The Public.

THE STATE OF TEXAS  )
COUNTY OF FREESTONE.) KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

That We, M. S. Bounds and wife Mattie Bounds of the county of Limestone and State of Texas, do hereby, under and by virtue of the laws of the State of Texas, in such cases made and provided, designate and set apart as our homestead the following described land in Limestone and Freestone County, Texas to-wit: 200 acres on the line of Freestone and Limestone Counties, State of Texas, in two tracts, and principally in Freestone County, Texas, and described by metes and bounds as follows:

FIRST TRACT: Being 20 acres principally in Limestone County, Texas, out of the Sarah McAnulty League, and beginning at the NW cor. of a three acres tract sold to J. H. Bounds by R. Manning, a point in Mexia-Wortham public road; THENCE North with said road 932.5 vrs stake for corner; THENCE East 207 vrs to H. & T. C. R. R. right-of-way; THENCE South with same to iron stake between John Manning and said right-of-way; THENCE West 34.5 vrs. to beginning.

SECOND TRACT: Out of the Wm Richie League, beginning at the NE corner of the 160 acres set apart to the McClelland heirs in partition of T. A. Bounds' Est.; THENCE N. 55 W. 980 vrs stake in line for corner; THENCE N. 35 E. 1047 vrs stake for corner in NE line of original 317.5 acres tract out of which this is taken; THENCE S. 55 E. 980 vrs with said line to corner; THENCE S. 35 W. 1047 vrs to place of beginning, and containing 180 acres of land. Both tracts containing two hundred (200) or more acres of land. And we do hereby declare that the said land is now owned, occupied, held and used by us as our homestead, that we actually reside thereon, and that our homestead claim does not embrace any other lands;

WITNESS our hands, this the 3d day of February A. D. 1934.

                                M. S. Bounds
                                Mattie Bounds

THE STATE OF TEXAS, )
COUNTY OF FREESTONE.) Before me W. J. Bryant in and for Freestone County, Texas, on this day personally appeared M. S. Bounds and Mattie Bounds known to me to be the persons whose names are subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me they executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed. And the said Mattie Bounds wife of M. S. Bounds having been examined by me privily and apart from her husband, and having had the same fully explained to her by me, she, the said Mattie Bounds acknowledged such instrument to be her act and deed, and declared that she had willingly signed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed, and that she did not wish to retract it.

Given under my hand and seal of office, this 8th day of February A. D. 1934.

                                W. J. Bryant, Notary Public,
(L. S.)                         Freestone County, Texas.

Filed for record on February 8th, 1934 at 9 o'clock a. m. and recorded February 8th, 1934 at 9:40 o'clock a. m.

                                Anna Burney, County Clerk,
                                Limestone County, Texas.

By [ILLEGIBLE]arten          Deputy.
