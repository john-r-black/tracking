HEIRSHIP AFFIDAVIT

STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF Limestone

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this the 2nd day
of August, 1994, appeared E. A. Strange Jr and
James Black each of whom deposes and says as follows:

1.  We were well acquainted with Marguerite Bounds prior to
her death and those heirs surviving her.

2.  Marguerite Bounds died in Wortham, Freestone County,
Texas at the age of seventy-six (76) on April 20, 1981.

3.  Marguerite Bounds was survived by two children: (a)
Hallye Rae Bounds Tyner of Tyler, Smith County, Texas and (b) Billy
Joe Bounds of Wortham, Freestone County, Texas.

4.  Billy Joe Bounds has three children as follows: (a) Joe
Edgar Bounds, who is married to Nancy Bounds, (b) Patricia Bounds
Vogel, who is married to Harvey Vogel, and (c) Bonnie Jean Bounds
Reeves, who is married to Norman Reeves.

5.  Hallye Rae Bounds Tyner has one daughter, Mitzi Dee
Tyner, a femme sole.

6.  All four of the above named grandchildren of Marguerite
Bounds survived the death of Billy Joe Bounds which occurred in
Falls County, Texas on the 28th day of March, 1993, and all such
grandchildren are living today.
