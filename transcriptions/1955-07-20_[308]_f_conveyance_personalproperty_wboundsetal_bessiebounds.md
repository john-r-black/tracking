THREET and husband, ELMER THREET, being the children of GEORGE P. BOUNDS, deceased, being beneficiaries under the will of JESSE VASTINE BOUNDS, JR., deceased, for and in consideration of ONE DOLLAR and the further consideration that when said will was executed on the 18th day of June, 1923, the said J.V. BOUNDS, JR. owned and possessed a great deal more personal property than he was seized with at the time of his death and that the personal property presently in his estate may be needed by his devoted wife and widow, MRS. BESSIE BOUNDS, to defray her actual and necessary expenses during the remainder of her life and because our beloved kinsman, J.V. BOUNDS, JR., who was ill for a considerable length of time before his death would probably have desired us to take this action under the circumstances and would approve said action on our part, do hereby give, grant, convey, set over and deliver to the said MRS. BESSIE BOUNDS all of our right, title and interest in and to all of the personal property vested in us under the terms of the will of JESSE VASTINE BOUNDS, JR., DECEASED.

THIS gift does not include and is without prejudice to out rights of remainder in the real estate vested in us under the terms of said will.
