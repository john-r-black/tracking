[MARGIN: J.H. Bounds / To Deed / J.E. Bounds]

The State of Texas } Know all men by these presents
County of Limestone } that I, J.H. Bounds of the above County & State have this day for and in consideration of the sum of one Dollar to me in hand paid by J.E. Bounds of the Same State & County given granted bargained and

[--- Page Break: DR-0000F-00585-02.png ---]

[STAMP: 586]

and sold and by these presents give grant bargain and sell to the said J.E. Bounds and his heirs and assigns forever the following tract or parcel of Land lying and being in said county & state and in the South east corner of the John Boyd League and Beginning at the South East corner of said John Boyd League a stake on a tangent Thence north with the east line of said John Boyd League to the middle of the Main Channel of Tehuacana creek thence up said said Tehuacana creek to a cluster of three live oak Trees for corner and a base of five hundred varas Thence South 197 varas to a stake in the South line of said John Boyd League thence East with the South line of the John Boyd League five (5) varas to the Beginning containing one hundred and sixty two acres more or less To have and to hold to him the said J.E. Bounds and his heirs and assigns forever and I the said J.H. Bounds hereby bind my self my heirs and assigns to warrant and forever defend the title to said described tract or parcel of land to the said J.E. Bounds and his heirs and assigns against the claims or claim of all persons claiming by or through myself or my heirs and against the claims of all persons whatsoever In testimony whereof I have hereto set my hand and affixed my seal this 7th day of October A.D. 1874

State of Texas }
Limestone County }                    J.H. Bounds (Seal)

Before me J.D. Walker Clerk of the County Court in and for said County personally appeared J.H. Bounds who is to me personally known and acknowledged that he had signed executed and delivered the foregoing instrument of writing for the purposes and consideration therein Specified Witness my official seal and signature at my office in the town of Groesbeck this 20th day of February A.D. 1877

J.D. Walker
Clerk C.C. L.C.

I hereby certify that the foregoing is a true copy of the original Deed and certificate of acknowledgement filed in my office for record [?:of] February [?:7]th 1877 at 9 1/2 oclock A.M. and duly recorded March 1st 1877 at 4 oclock P.M.

[?:J].D. Walker
