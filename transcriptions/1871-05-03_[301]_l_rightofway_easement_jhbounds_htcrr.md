[MARGIN: J.H. Bounds / to Hou / H T C.R.R.Co]

The State of Texas Know all Men by these Presents That for and in Consideration of the Enhanced Value to be given, and is contemplated to arise to my lands & other property by the location & building of the Houston & Texas Central Railroad & for the further consideration of Three Hundred & Seventy five coin Dollars to me in hand paid the receipt whereof is hereby acknowledged I James H. Bounds of the County of Limestone & State of Texas have granted bargained sold & released & by these presents do grant bargain sell & release to the Houston & Texas Central Railway Company a strip of 200 feet in width of land over the tracts of land particularly described as follows to wit, in Limestone County Texas on the Hall tract a part of the Sarah W. McNulty league lying & being situated in Limestone County, State of Texas upon the waters of Tehuacana Creek having been conveyed to me by T.J. Hall and wife by deed dated 15th Dec 1865 and upon which the said company has built or may hereafter build its Railroad &d also the right to have the same strip of land in width, over & upon any other lands now owned or which may hereafter be owned by me in this State, through which said Railroad has been or may be hereafter

built; together with all & singular the rights Members hereditaments unto and appurtenances to the same belonging or in anywise incident or appertaining To have & to hold, all & singular, the said premises unto the said Houston & Texas Central Railway Company or its assigns forever. And for the considerations aforesaid as herein set forth I do further grant to said Company such Earth Material timber & stone as may be found on the lands herein mentioned which may be required for the construction of said Railroad. And I do hereby grant to said Company a full release from all claims against said Company for damages that may be sustained by their work in the construction & for the right of way of said Railroad over any of the said lands. Witness my hand & seal this 3rd day of May A.D. 1871

Witness                    James H. Bounds  (Seal)
L. P. Tutt
John A. Quinn

The State of Texas }
Before me J.B. Vallandingham clerk of the District Court in & for Limestone County & State of Texas this day personally appeared L. P. Tutt to me known & acknowledged that he saw James H. Bounds sign the foregoing Deed of Conveyance for all the purpose and considerations therein stated & that he signed the same as witness at the request of said Grantor Given under my hand & seal this the 3rd day May 1871.

J.B. Vallandingham Clk D.C. & C.C.
