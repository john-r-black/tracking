J. H. Bounds & wife to deed J. W. Bounds

WARRANTY DEED, with Joint Acknowledgment—Geo. D. Barnard & Co., Blank Book Manufacturers, Printers, Lithographers, and Stationers, St. Louis.

The State of Texas,
COUNTY OF Limestone Know all Men by these Presents:
THAT We J. H. Bounds & M. E. Bounds husband and wife of the County of Limestone and State aforesaid, for and in consideration of the sum of five DOLLARS, to us in hand paid by J. W. Bounds of Limestone County Texas and for the further consideration of the natural love and affection we have for our Son (the said J. W. Bounds) the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged,

have GRANTED, SOLD AND CONVEYED, and by these Presents do GRANT, SELL AND CONVEY, unto the said J. W. Bounds of the County of Limestone and State of Texas all that certain All that certain lot or parcel of land situated and being in the County of Limestone in the State of Texas as part of the John Boyd Survey, and described as follows; beginning at a point 500 varas, west of the South East corner, of said John Boyd League a stake in the south line of said League, thence north (with) J. H. Bounds west line to Tehuacana Creek, about 1900 vrs, thence up said Creek with its meanderings to the north East corner of a 150 acre tract sold by Foster to Shown and by Shown to Atkinson and Foster, thence South 1664 vrs, to a stake in the south line of said Boyd League and the south east corner of the above refered to 150 acre tract, thence East with said Boyds south line 500 varas, to the place of beginning containing one hundred and fifty eight acres 158 acres) more or less, less two acres sold, one to J.H. Sparks off the south west corner, and one to the State of Tex as off of the south east corner,

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto belonging, unto the said J. W. Bounds his heirs and assigns, forever. And We do hereby bind ourselves our heirs, executors and administrators, to Warrant and Forever Defend, all and singular, the said premises unto the said J. W. Bounds his and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same, or any part thereof.
WITNESS our hands at Wortham, this 27th day of March A. D. 1889

Signed and delivered in presence of
J. H. Bounds
M. E. Bounds

THE STATE OF TEXAS,
County of Freestone BEFORE ME, J. E. Longbotham a Notary Public in and for Freestone County, Texas, on this day personally appeared J. H. Bounds and M. E. Bounds wife of J. H. Bounds both Known to me to be the persons whose names are subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that they executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed. And the said M. E. Bounds wife of the said J. H. Bounds having been examined by me privily and apart from her husband, and having the same by me fully explained to her, the said M. E. Bounds acknowledged such instrument to me to be her act and deed, and she declared that she had willingly signed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed, and that she did not wish to retract it.
GIVEN under my hand and seal of office, this 27th day of March A. D. 1889
(L.S.)
J. E. Longbotham N. P.
Freestone Co. Texas

THE STATE OF TEXAS,
County of Limestone W. F.
