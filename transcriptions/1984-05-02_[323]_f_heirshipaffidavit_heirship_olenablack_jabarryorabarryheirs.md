AFFIDAVIT OF HEIRSHIP

THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF FREESTONE

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared [handwritten: Olena Black], known to me to be a credible person, above the age of eighteen years, and not disqualified in any way, and deposed as follows:

That she was well and personally acquainted with John Adam Barry and Ora Barry during their lifetimes; that they were married but one time, that marriage being to each other; that to this marriage were born five children, Dorothy Barry Garland, Kate Barry Mays, Annie Barry Wilson, Jesse Louise Barry Harvey, and a child who died as an infant; and that the said John Adam Barry and Ora Barry did not care for any other children in their home.

That John Adam Barry died in Freestone County, Texas, on August 27, 1939, intestate, leaving as his survivors, his wife and four children, Dorothy Barry Garland, Kate Barry Mays, Annie Barry Wilson and Jesse Louise Barry Harvey, and no other; that no administration was had on his estate, and none was necessary as all legal debts and obligations against his estate were discharged by his heirs at law; and further that no Federal Estate Tax or State Inheritance Tax was due on his estate since the estate was not of sufficient size to be liable for said taxes.

That Ora Barry died in Limestone County, Texas on September 26, 1961, intestate, leaving as her survivors, her children, Dorothy Barry Garland, Kate Barry Mays, Annie Barry Wilson and Jesse Louise Barry Harvey, and no other; that no administration was had on her estate, and none was necessary as all legal debts and obligations against her estate were discharged by her heirs at law; and further that no Federal Estate Tax or State Inheritance Tax was due on her estate since the estate was not of sufficient size to be liable for said taxes.
