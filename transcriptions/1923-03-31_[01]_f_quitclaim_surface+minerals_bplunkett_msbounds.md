DEED

BESSIE PLUNKETT ET AL TO M.S. BOUNDS
THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF FREESTONE 

Know all men by these presents, that we, Bessie Plunkett, a widow of the county of Navarro, state of Texas; R.H. Lee and wife Bessie Lee, L.M. Irving, and wife Stella Irving, of the county of Freestone, state of Texas, for an in consideration of the sum of one dollar and other valuable consideration, to us in hand paid by M.S. Bounds, of the county of Freestone, state of Texas, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, do, by these presents, bargain, sell, release and forever quitclaim unto the said M.S. Bounds his heirs and assigns, all our right, title and interest in and to that certain tract or parcel of land lying in the county of Limestone and in the county of Freestone, in the State of Texas, described as follows:

FIRST TRACT: Beginning at the northeast corner of the J.H. Bounds homestead tract, rock for corner from which P.O. 9.00 in. dia. brs. S10°00'00"E 2.00 vrs.;

THENCE S 1020.00 vrs. to a stake in the center of a ravine;

THENCE with meanderings of the said ravine S32°00'00"W 32.00 vrs., S67°00'00"W 67.00 vrs., S17°00'00"E 62.00 vrs., S60°00'00"E 43.00 vrs., S21°00'00"E 31.00 vrs., S22°00'00"W 39.00 vrs., N78°00'00"W 50.00 vrs., S10°00'00"E 63.00 vrs., S74°00'00"W 31.00 vrs., S39°00'00"E 50.00 vrs.;

THENCE S 467.00 vrs. to S W corner of the Tom Bounds 114.00 acre tract on N line of Beckham tract;

THENCE West with said Beckham line 430.00 vrs. to East line of H&TCRR right of way;

THENCE N12°00'00"E with the right of way of said RR 1740.00 vrs. to the north line of said Bounds homestead tract;

THENCE S88°00'00"E with said line 194.00 vrs. to the beginning, containing 94.00 acres of land.


SECOND TRACT: Being a three acres tract deeded by Reuben Manning to J.H. Bounds, dated July 20, 1869, recorded in vol G, page 661, deed records of Limestone County, Texas to which reference is here made, this tract is taken at two acres in this deed more or less, being diminished by R R right of way.


THIRD TRACT: Being part of the S. McAnulty survey partly in Freestone and Limestone Counties, being twenty acres on the west side of the H&TCRR and bounded on the south by J.H. Bounds land and west by the Mexia and Wortham road, and for a more particular description as follows:

Being twenty acres, the greater part in Limestone County, beginning at the NW cor of a 3.00 acre tract sold to J H Bounds by R Manning, a point in Mexia and Wortham road;

THENCE N with said road 932.50 vrs. stake, whence a honey locust 16.00 in. brs. S11°00'00"W 24.50 vrs. and an elm 5.00 in. brs. S28°00'00"E 23.00 vrs.

THENCE EAST 207.00 vrs. to H&TCRR right of way.

THENCE S with said right of way to an iron stake between John Manning and said right of way.

THENCE W 34.50 vrs. to the place of beginning; a tract of 2.00 acres is reserved out of S E corner of said 94.00 acres having been sold to Negro New Home Church, making this deed contain in the aggregate 114.00 acres.

To have and to hold the said premises, together with all and singular the rights, privileges and appurtenances thereto in any manner belonging unto the said M.S. Bounds, his heirs and assigns, forever, so that we, nor our heirs, nor any person or persons claiming under us shall, at any time hereafter, have, claim or demand any right of title to the aforesaid premises or appurtenances, or any part thereof.

Witness our hands, this the _ day of March A.D. 1923.
R.H. Lee
Bessie Lee
Bessie Plunkett
Lewis M. Irvin
Stella Irvin

THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF WICHITA

Given under my hand and seal of office this the 19 day of March AD 1927.
