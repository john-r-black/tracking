[J. O. Longbotham
to } Deed
J. H. Bounds

The State of Texas County of Freestone} Know all men by these presents. that I J. O. Longbotham. of the State & county aforesaid, for and in consideration of the sum of seventy five and 50/100 Dollars to me in hand paid by J. H. Bounds of the County of Limestone State aforesaid, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged. have sold and conveyed and by these presents do sell and convey unto said J. H. Bounds seven and one half acres of land described particularly as follows

Beginning 401 3/4 vrs. west of the S. W. corner of a 693 acre tract belonging to Jno. Manning, all being situated on the McAnulty League. at a stake in the north boundary line of J. H. Bounds 278 acre survey. thence north 105 vrs a stake whence P.O. 14 in. brs S19 E. 6/4 vrs & P.O. 12 in. brs E. 1 1/4 vrs. thence west 401 1/4 vrs a stake in a dugout thence south 105 vrs. to J. H. Bounds north line. thence east with said north line 401 1/4 vrs to the place of beginning, all in Limestone County — To have and to hold the said described land unto the said J. H. Bounds his heirs and assigns forever, and I J. O. Longbotham do hereby covenant and agree, for myself and my heirs to forever warrant and defend the title to the said land unto the said J. H. Bounds and heirs and assigns against the lawful and just claims of any and all persons claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof.

Witness my hand this 6th day of December A.D. 1881.
J. O. Longbotham
M. J. Longbotham

The State of Texas }
County of Freestone } Before me Warren Allegra a Justice of the Peace and Ex officio Notary Public in and for said County on this day personally appeared J. O. Longbotham Known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing Deed, and acknowledged to me that he executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed.

Given under my hand and seal of office this 6th day of December A.D. 1881.
(L.S.)
Warren Allegra J.P. & Ex off.
A.P. Freestone Co.

The State of Texas }
County of Freestone } Before me Warren Allegra a Justice of the Peace & Ex off. Notary Public in & for said County personally appeared M. J. Longbotham, wife of J. O. Longbotham Known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing Deed, and after being examined by me privily & apart from her said husband & having the same by me fully explained to her she the said M. J. Longbotham acknowledged such instrument to be her own act and deed, and declared that she has willingly signed the same, and that she did not wish to retract.

Given under my hand and seal of office this 24th day of December A.D. 1881.
(L.S.)
Warren Allegra
J.P. & Ex off. N.P.
Freestone County Texas

I hereby certify that the foregoing is a true and correct copy of the original Deed and certificate of acknowledgement filed for record April 8th 1882 at 5. oclock Pm. and duly recorded April 10th 1882 at 3. oclock Pm.
S. D. Walker Co. Clk.
R. Wiley Deputy
