MICHAEL HOOKS, SANDRA BOUNDS MICK, joined pro forma by husband RICHARD A.
MICK, P. O. Box 856, Wortham, TX 76693 ("GRANTOR"), for and in consideration of the sum of TEN
AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($10.00), and other good and valuable consideration to him in hand paid by
TEXAS UTILITIES ELECTRIC COMPANY, Energy Plaza, 1601 Bryan St., Dallas, TX 75201,
("GRANTEE"), the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, has granted, sold and
conveyed, and by these presents does grant, sell and convey unto the GRANTEE all that certain property
lying and being situated in Freestone County, Texas, more particularly described in Exhibit "A" and
depicted on Exhibit "B" attached hereto and made a part hereof.

It is understood and agreed that GRANTOR, their heirs, successors and assigns shall be responsible
for the erection and maintenance of its electric fence to be built on the new surveyed boundary line of the
property described in Exhibit "A". GRANTEE shall be responsible for erection and maintenance of its fence
within the limits of the property described in Exhibit "A".

It is further understood and agreed to that GRANTOR reserves all oil, gas, and other minerals
located in, on and under the property described in Exhibit "A" herein. GRANTOR conveys to GRANTEE
all interest in coal and lignite located in, on and under the property described in Exhibit "A" herein.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above-described premises, together with all and singular the rights
and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said TEXAS UTILITIES ELECTRIC
COMPANY so long as said premises are used for a rail line to serve GRANTEE's Big Brown Steam
Electric Station and GRANTEE abandons said premises; and it is expressly understood that in the event
the premises hereinabove described permanently cease to be used for rail transportation to serve Big Brown
Steam Electric Station and GRANTEE abandons said premises, then in that event, this conveyance shall
be null and void and the said premises, shall absolutely revert to the GRANTOR herein, his heirs,
successors, or assigns, without the necessity of any other act or action on the part of the GRANTOR,
