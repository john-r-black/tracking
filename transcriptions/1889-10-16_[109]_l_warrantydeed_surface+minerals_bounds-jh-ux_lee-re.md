The State of Texas County of Limestone } Know all men by these presents that we J. H. Bounds and wife M. E. of the county of Limestone state of Texasin consideration of the sum of six hundred and ninety dollars paid and secured to be paid by R. E. Lee as follows: four promissory notes each payable respectively Jan 1st 1892 for 172 50/100 Jan 1st 1892 for $172 50/100, Jan 1st 1893 for $172 50/100 and Jan 1st 1894 for $172 50/100 have granted bargained sold and conveyed and by these presents do grant bargain sell and convey unto the said R. E. Lee of the county of Limestone and state of Texas all that certain lot tract or parcel of land described as follows: a part of a 99 acre tract a part of the Samuel Holloway survey situated in the corners of Limestone, Freestone and Navarro Counties in the state of Texas this part being the S. E. and N. portion of said 99 acre tract and containing 69 acres of land more or less said 99 acre tract being more minutely described in said J. H. Bounds and M. E. Bounds deed recorded in book "M" deed records of Limestone County pages 390, 391 and 392. Together with all and singular the rights members hereditaments and appurtenances to the same belonging or in anywise incident or appertaining. To have and to hold all and singular the said premises unto the said R. E. Lee his heirs and assigns forever, and we hereby bind our heirs executors and administrators to warrant and forever defend all and singular the said premises unto the said R. E. Lee his heirs and assigns against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof, But it is expressly agreed and stipulated that the vendors lien is retained against the above described property premises and improvements until the above described notes and all interest thereon are fully paid according to the face tenor and effect when this deed shall became absolute. Witness our hands this 16th day of October A.D. 1889.
J. H. Bounds
M. E. Bounds

The State of Texas }
County of Freestone } Before me J. E. Longbotham a Notary Public in and for Freestone County Texas on this day personally appeared J. H. Bounds and M. E. Bounds wife of J. H. Bounds and wife M. E. Bounds Known to me to be the persons whose names are subscribed to the foregoing instrument and acknowledged to me that they executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed, and the said M. E. Bounds wife of the said J. H. Bounds having been examined by me privily and apart from her husband, and having the same fully explained to her she the said M. E. Bounds acknowledged each instrument to be her act and deed and declared that she had willingly signed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed and that she did not wish to retract it. Given under my hand and seal of office this 16th day of Oct. 1889.
(L.S.)
J. E. Longbotham N. P.
Freestone Co. Tex.

I hereby certify that the foregoing is a true and correct copy of the original Deed and certificate of acknowledgement, filed in my office for record March 27th 1890 at 9 oclock A.M. and duly recorded March 26th 1890 at 3 P.M.
W. F. Brown co. clk.
By J. D. Brown Dept.
