QUITCLAIM DEED

STATE OF TEXAS )
COUNTY OF FREESTONE )

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

THAT for and in consideration of Ten Dollars ($10.00) and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, the undersigned OLENA BOUNDS BLACK, of Freestone County, Texas, HAS QUITCLAIMED, and by these presents DOES QUITCLAIM unto the JOHN PIERCE BLACK FAMILY TRUST OF 1977, a Texas trust, dated July 19, 1977, all of her right, title, and interest in and to the following described real property situated in Freestone County, Texas, to-wit:

All that certain tract of land containing 85.30 acres, more or less, out of the Sarah McAnulty Survey, and being the same land described in a Deed dated October 18, 1943, from Mrs. A.H. Edwards, et al., to J. P. Black, and recorded in Volume 168, Page 588, Deed Records, Freestone County, Texas, reference to which is hereby made for all purposes.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD all of her right, title, and interest in and to the said premises, together with, all and singular, the rights, privileges, and appurtenances thereto in any manner belonging unto the said OLENA BOUNDS BLACK, her heirs and assigns forever, so that neither she nor her heirs, legal representatives, nor any person or persons claiming under her shall at any time hereafter have, claim, or demand any right or title to the aforesaid property, premises or appurtenances, or any part thereof.

Executed this 1st day of October, 1979.
Olena Bounds Black


(Acknowledgment)

STATE OF Texas )
COUNTY OF Freestone )

GIVEN UNDER MY HAND AND SEAL OF OFFICE this the 1st day of October, 1979.
