The State of Texas
County of Limestone

Know All Men by These Presents:
That I, M. S. BOUNDS of the County of LIMESTONE State of TEXAS for and in consideration of the sum of ONE ($1.00) DOLLARS to me in hand paid by OLENA BLACK, as her separate property as follows:

Cash receipt of which is here acknowledged, and the further consideration of the love and affection that I have for my daughter, OLENA BLACK 
have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and Convey unto the said OLENA BLACK of the County of Limestone State of Texas all that certain tract of land situated in the County of Freestone, State of Texas, described as follows, to-wit:
 
A part of the Snapp pasture out of the Wm. Richie league and 
Beginning at the N.E. cor. of the 160.00 acres tract set apart to the minor heirs of Laura McClelland, dec'd.,

Thence N55°00'00"W 1720.00 vrs. a stake in the E. line of the tract owned by W. S. Evans,

Thence N50°00'00"E 950.00 vrs. corner.

Thence N55°00'00"W 344.00 vrs. Thence N35°00'00"E 270.00 vrs., stake in Evans E. line;

Thence S55°00'00"E 1856.00 vrs., stake in Wortham and Streetman road.

Thence S35°00'00"E 1074.00 vrs. to the beginning, containing 317.50 acres of land. 

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said OLENA BLACK, her heirs and assigns forever and I do hereby bind myself, my heirs, executors and administrators, to Warrant and Forever Defend, all and singular the said premises unto the said heirs and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming, or to claim the same, or any part thereof.

WITNESS my hand at Wortham, Texas this seventh day of January 1972


SINGLE ACKNOWLEDGMENT
THE STATE OF TEXAS,
COUNTY OF FREESTONE
BEFORE ME, the undersigned, a Notary Public in and for said County and State, on this day personally appeared M. S. BOUNDS known to me to be the person whose name is subscribed to the foregoing instrument, and acknowledged to me that he executed the same for the purposes and consideration therein expressed.
GIVEN UNDER MY HAND AND SEAL OF OFFICE this the 22 day of September A.D. 1972
Harry P Bounds
Notary Public in and for Freestone County, Texas
My commission expires June 1, 1973
