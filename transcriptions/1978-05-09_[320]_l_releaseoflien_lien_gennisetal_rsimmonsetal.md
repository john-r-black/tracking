**RELEASE OF LIEN**

THE STATE OF TEXAS        }
COUNTY OF                 }   KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

THAT the undersigned, of the Counties of Harris, Freestone, Limestone and State of Texas,
the legal and equitable owner and holder of that one certain promissory note in the original principal sum
of Twelve Thousand Eight Hundred Dollars ($12,800.00)
dated January 18, 1975, executed by Roy Simmons and wife, Virginia M.
Simmons,
payable to the order of Georgia Nell Ennis, Genevieve Dorsett and John P. Black,
more fully described in a Deed of Trust, duly recorded in Vol. 94
page 534 of the Deed of Trust Records of Limestone County, Texas;
said note being secured by
against the following described property, to-wit: All of the following described property
in Limestone County, Texas, consisting of tracts set forth herein, to-wit:
FIRST TRACT: Being a part of the John Boyd Survey, Limestone County, Texas,
and described as follows:
BEGINNING at the Southwest corner of the two acre lot known as the Quaite
2 acres, fronting on Street in the town of Tehuacana;
THENCE South with said Street 152-3/5 varas to the Northwest corner of a 3.6
acre tract conveyed by D. B. Hornbeck to Mrs. C. D. Hornbeck on the 9th
day of November, 1912, a stake;
THENCE East 238.4 varas the northern Northeast corner of said 3.6 acre tract;
THENCE South about 10 varas to rock for corner; THENCE East 199 varas;
THENCE South 63 East 63-3/5 varas to the W. line of the Erwin tract;
THENCE North 5 West with said Erwin line 281-3/5 varas to the South line of
the Tom Bounds tract;
THENCE 89-1/4 W. with said Bounds S. line 350-3/5 varas to the N.E.
corner of said Quaite lot;
THENCE S. 83-4/5 varas to the S. E. corner of said lot;
THENCE West with the S. line of said lot to the place of beginning 130-4/5
varas and containing 16.8 acres of land, more or less.
SECOND TRACT: Being a part of the John Boyd Survey and described as follows:
BEGINNING at the South line of the A. A. Davis tract which point is also the
N. E. corner of the Mrs. N. J. Wade tract;
THENCE South 88 deg. 30' East with the South line of said Davis tract, 773.6
varas to N. W. corner of the old Geo. A. Neill tract;
THENCE with the said Neill's line South 4 deg. East 187 varas a stake for cor.;
THENCE North 88 deg. 30' West 775.5 varas, a stake for corner;
THENCE North 1 deg. 30' West 182 varas to the place of beginning, containing
25 acres of land, more or less.
THIERD TRACT: Being a part of the John Boyd Survey and described as follows:
BEGINNING 131-1/8 feet South of Block No. 2 known as the A. J. Collins home
place and running 100 feet South the J. S. Miller's N. E. corner;
THENCE Due East with J. A. Miller's North line 374 feet;
THENCE North 100 feet to a stake for corner;
THENCE West 374 feet to the place of beginning and containing one acre more
or less.
Less a lot containing 100 feet by 100 feet previously deeded by John T. Black
and Grantor.

for and in consideration of the full and final payment of all indebtedness secured by the aforesaid lien or liens,
the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, has released and discharged, and by these presents hereby releases
and discharges, the above described property from all liens held by the undersigned securing said indebtedness.

EXECUTED this 9th day of May, A. D. 19 78

Georgia Nell Ennis
Genevieve Dorsett
John P. Black
