The State of Texas, County of Limestone

Know All Men by These Presents:

That I, M. S. Bounds of the County of Limestone State of Texas for and in consideration of the sum of One and No/100 Dollars ($1.00) and the love and affection I have for my daughter, to me in hand paid by Olena Black as follows:

Cash, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, 

have Granted, Sold and Conveyed, and by these presents do Grant, Sell and Convey, unto the said Olena Black the County of Limestone State of Texas all that certain 224.00 acres of land in Limestone and Freestone Counties, Texas, out of the Sarah McAnulty Survey, A-19, and being five tracts of land, described with particularity as follows:

THENCE S 467.00 vrs. to the S.W. corner of the Tom Bounds 114.00 acre tract on N. line of Beckham tract;

THENCE West with said Beckham line 430.00 vrs. to the East line of H & T.C.RR. Right of way;

THENCE N12°00'00"E with the right of way of said R.R. 1740.00 vrs. to the North line of said Bounds homestead tract;

SECOND TRACT: 2.00 acres described as follows:
Being a 3.00 acre tract deeded by Rueben Manning to J. H. Bounds, dated July 20th 1869, said deed recorded in Volume G, page 661 of the Deed Records of Limestone County, Texas, to which deed and the records reference is here made, this tract is taken at 2.00 acres in this deed more or less, being diminished by the R.R. Right of Way.


THIRD TRACT: Being partly in Freestone and Limestone County, and being 20.00 acres out of the West side of the H & T.C.RR. beginning at the N.W. corner of a 3.00 acre tract sold to J.H. Bounds by R. Manning a point in the Mexia and Wortham road;

THENCE N with said road 932.50 vrs. a stake whence a honey locust 16.00 in. brs. S11°00'00"W 24.50 vrs. and an elm 5.00 in. brs. S28°00'00"E 23.00 vrs.;

THENCE E 207.00 vrs. to H & T.C.RR.R/W;

THENCE W 34.50 vrs. to the place of beginning, containing 20.00 acres.


FOURTH TRACT: BEGINNING at the N.W. corner of the A.H. Edwards tract in the division line of the Sarah McAnulty League;

THENCE S 357.00 vrs. to Tehuacana Creek;

THENCE up said creek with its meanders, to the Mouth of Elm Branch as changed by Arch Smith;

THENCE up said branch S10°00'00"W 172.00 vrs., S25°00'00"W 36.00 vrs., S85°00'00"W 61.00 vrs., S20°00'00"E 36.00 vrs., S 72.00 vrs., N35°00'00"E 59.00 vrs., S50°00'00"E 36.00 vrs., S60°00'00"W 44.00 vrs., S20°00'00"W 51.00 vrs., N83°00'00"W 60.00 vrs., N25°00'00"E 21.00 vrs., W 36.00 vrs., S42°00'00"W 61.00 vrs., N69°00'00"W 29.00 vrs., S15°00'00"E 52.00 vrs., S75°00'00"W 61.00 vrs., S10°00'00"W 144.00 vrs., N75°00'00"E 72.00 vrs., S42°00'00"W 26.00 vrs., S 36.00 vrs., S37°00'00"W 14.00 vrs. cor;

FIFTH TRACT: 18.00 acres out of the said survey and lying on the W. side of the H & TC RR Right of Way, and being all of the land owned by the Estate of T.A. Bounds on said W. side of railroad in said survey, said land now being in possession of M.S. Bounds.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the above described premises, together with all and singular the rights and appurtenances thereto in anywise belonging unto the said Olena Black, her heirs and assigns forever and I do hereby bind myself, my 
heirs, executors and administrators, to Warrant and Forever Defend, all and singular the said premises unto the said Olena Black, her heirs and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming, or to claim the same, or any part thereof.

WITNESS my hand at Wortham, Texas this second day of October, 1972.
M. S. Bounds
