PARTITION DEED 

THE STATE OF TEXAS
COUNTY OF LIMESTONE

RECITALS 
The Estate of Olena Bounds Black, Deceased, by and through its duly appointed Independent Co-Executors, JOHN MARION BLACK and JAMES ALLEN BLACK, and JOHN MARION BLACK, individually, acknowledge their common ownership of the property described as Tract One and Tract Two below and desire to partition the property between them.

Therefore, considering the premises and to effect the partition, the parties to this Deed covenant, grant, conclude, and agree between themselves and for their heirs, successors, and assigns that a partition of the property be made as follows:
