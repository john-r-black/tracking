[ANNOTATION: 1580 317 (top center, handwritten)]

THE STATE OF TEXAS

COUNTY OF LIMESTONE

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared Kelly Bounds and Jeff Bounds, affiants, and on their oaths say that they are each over twenty-one years of age and are not incapacitated in any way; and that the following facts are true and correct: That affiants were each well and personally acquainted with John T. Black during his said life time; that the said John T. Black was married one time and one time only and that was to Verona E. Pierce, and that the said Verona E. Pierce was married one time and one time only and that was to John T. Black; that to the marriage of John T. Black and Verona E. Black were born the following children and no others,viz:

1. J. P. Black, a son, who presently lives in Limestone County, Texas.
2. Georgia Nell Black, a daughter, who is married to R. A. Ennis; that the said Georgia Nell Ennis and R. A. Ennis presently reside in Harris County, Texas.
3. Genevieve Black, a daughter, who is married to L. L. Dorsett; that the said Genevieve Dorsett and L. L. Dorsett presently reside in Dallas County, Texas;

that the said John T. Black did not adopt any children during his said life time. Affiants further state that John T. Black died intestate on January 30, 1959, and that there is no administration of his estate pending nor is there a necessity therefor; that the estate of the said John T. Black would have appraised at less than $10,000.00; that John T. Black and J. T. Black are one and the same person and name.

WITNESS OUR HANDS, this 9th day of February, A. D. 1959.

[handwritten signature: Kelly Bounds]
[handwritten signature: Jeff Bounds]
