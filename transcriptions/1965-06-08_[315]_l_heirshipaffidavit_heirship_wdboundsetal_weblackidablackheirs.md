THE STATE OF TEXAS

COUNTY OF LIMESTONE

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, on this day personally appeared W. D. Bounds and Jeff Bounds, affiants, and on their oaths say that they are each over twenty-one years of age and are not incapacitated in any way; and that the following facts are true and correct: That affiants were each well and personally acquainted with W. E. Black and wife, Ida A. Black during their lifetimes; that the said W. E. Black and Ida A. Black were married one time and one time only and that was to each other; that to the union of W. E. Black and Ida A. Black were born the following children and no others, viz:

1. Hallie Black, a daughter, who married J. R. Milburn.
2. Ella Black, a daughter, who married George Rowell, also known as G. L. Rowell.
3. Minnie Black, a daughter, who married W. D. Swint.
4. Zona Black, a daughter, who married P. Thompson, also known as F. P. Thompson.
5. C. E. Black, a son, whose wife was Erna Black.
6. J. A. Black, a son, whose wife in 1943 was named Leta Black; that J. A. Black was living in the State of Arizona on January 2, 1943, and was separated from his wife, Leta Black at that time.
7. J. T. Black, also known as John T. Black, a son, whose wife was Verona E. Black.

That neither W. E. Black nor Ida A. Black adopted any child or children during their said lifetimes; that the said Ida A. Black, died,intestate, prior to the year 1943 and there was no administration had on her estate nor a necessity therefor and that the value of her estate would not have exceeded $10,000.00; that the said W. E. Black had not remarried on January 2, 1943; that each

[ANNOTATION: 306 (left margin, handwritten repeat)]

of the above named children were over the age of 21 years on January 2, 1943.

WITNESS OUR HANDS, this the 8th day of June, A. D. 1965.

W D Bounds
Jeff Bounds
