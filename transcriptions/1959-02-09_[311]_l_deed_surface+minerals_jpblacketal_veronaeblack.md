THE STATE OF TEXAS    }
COUNTY OF LIMESTONE   }

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

THAT WE, J. P. Black, of the County of Limestone, State of Texas, Georgia Nell Ennis and husband, R. A. Ennis, of the County of Harris, State of Texas, and Genevieve Dorsett and husband L. L. Dorsett, of the County of Dallas, State of Texas, for and in consideration of the love and affection which we have and bear for Verona E. Black, mother of the said J. P. Black, Georgia Nell Ennis and Genevieve Dorsett, have given, granted and conveyed, and by these presents do give, grant and convey to the said Verona E. Black, of the County of Limestone and State of Texas, all of our interest in the estate of John T. Black, deceased, father of the grantors, J. P. Black, Georgia Nell Ennis and Genevieve Dorsett, including all personal property and real estate of every kind and character, whether situated in Limestone County, Texas, or any other county or state, it being the intention of grantors to convey to the grantee all of the interest which grantors inherited from the said John T. Black, deceased, including all real and personal property owned by the said John T. Black at the time of his death, regardless of where said property may be located, together with all and singular the rights, members and appurtenances to the same in any manner belonging.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD said property to her, the said Verona E. Black, her heirs and assigns forever.

WITNESS OUR HANDS, this 9th day of February, A. D. 1959.

[handwritten:] [signature]
J. P. Black
[handwritten:] [signature]
Georgia Nell Ennis
[handwritten:] [signature]
R. A. Ennis
[handwritten:] [signature]
Genevieve Dorsett
[handwritten:] [signature]
L. L. Dorsett
