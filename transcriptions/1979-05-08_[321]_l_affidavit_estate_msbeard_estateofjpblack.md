**AFFIDAVIT**

THE STATE OF TEXAS *
COUNTY OF McLENNAN *

BEFORE ME, the undersigned authority, a Notary Public in and for said County and State, on this day personally appeared M. STEPHEN BEARD, who being by me first duly sworn upon his oath deposes and says:

1. That he is a duly licensed and practicing attorney in Waco, McLennan County, Texas.

2. That he is the Attorney of Record for the Estate of John Pierce Black, Deceased.

3. That he has prepared and filed the State Inheritance Tax Return for and on behalf of the Estate of John Pierce Black, Deceased, and has received on behalf of the said estate, the Certificate from the Comptroller of Public Accounts, showing no inheritance tax to be due, as well as a Certificate from the Comptroller of Public Accounts releasing the Independent Executrix of the estate from personal liability, copies of both certificates are attached hereto and marked as Exhibit "A" and Exhibit "B", respectively, and incorporated herein for all purposes as though they were fully set forth at length herein.

4. That there was no Federal Estate Tax liability on said Estate.

[handwritten signature]
M. STEPHEN BEARD
