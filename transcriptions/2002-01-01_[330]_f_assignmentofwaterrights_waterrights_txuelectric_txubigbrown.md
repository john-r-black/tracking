ASSIGNMENT OF WATER RIGHTS

THE STATE OF TEXAS       §
                         §
COUNTY OF FREESTONE      §

WHEREAS, contemporaneously herewith TXU Electric Company, which is in the
process of changing its name to TXU US Energy Holding Company and which was formerly
known as Texas Utilities Electric Company and which is successor by merger to Dallas Power &
Light Company, Texas Power & Light Company, and Texas Electric Service Company
("Grantor"), pursuant to the requirements of the Utilities Code of the State of Texas, in order to
effectuate the provisions of that certain Master Separation Agreement dated January 1, 2002 to
which Grantor is a party, variously conveyed all of Grantor's right, title and interest in and to all
of its real property situated in Freestone County, Texas by special warranty deed ("Deed") to
TXU Big Brown Company LP, a Texas limited partnership ("BB Genco"), and others,
respectively; and

WHEREAS, evidence of the transfer of certain Water Rights, hereinafter defined,
associated with the BB Genco Property located in Freestone County, Texas, defined in said Deed
and conveyed to BB Genco thereby, is subject to being filed with the Texas Natural Resource
Conservation Commission.

NOW, THEREFORE, KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS: That Grantor, for
good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged
by Grantor, has GRANTED, ASSIGNED, BARGAINED, SOLD and CONVEYED, and by
these presents does hereby GRANT, ASSIGN, BARGAIN, SELL, and CONVEY, unto BB
Genco, whose address is 1601 Bryan, Dallas, Texas 75201, effective as of the effective time of
the Deed, all of Grantor's right, title and interest in and to the rights to the use of water and to
maintain existing dams and reservoirs under the Certificate(s) of Adjudication described in
Exhibit "A", attached hereto and made a part hereof for all purposes (collectively, the "Water
Rights").

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the Water Rights to BB Genco, and BB Genco's successors
and assigns forever, and Grantor does hereby bind Grantor and Grantor's successors to warrant
and forever defend, all and singular, the Water Rights unto BB Genco, and BB Genco's
successors and assigns, against every person whomsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the
same, or any part thereof by, through or under Grantor, but not otherwise.
