  - **Genevieve Dorsett** (4/4/1913–4/5/1999) — JP Black's sister; died in Tehuacana, Limestone County, Texas
  - **Georgia Nell Ennis** (5/1/1915–1/15/2008) — JP Black's sister; died in San Antonio, Bexar County, Texas
